How to Read Props

Hover over any term below for a quick explanation in plain English.

EV
EV (Expected Value)
A quick way to measure whether a bet looks worth taking. Positive EV means the model thinks the price may be better than it should be.
Model Probability
Model Probability
The model’s estimate of how often this prop should hit.
Line
Line
The number set by the sportsbook. Your bet usually depends on whether the player finishes over or under this number.
Odds
Odds
The payout price for the bet. Bigger plus-money odds pay more, but usually hit less often.
Bet Type
Bet Type
Shows whether the prop is an over, under, or milestone-style bet.
