How to Read Game Odds

Hover over any term below for a quick explanation in plain English.

EV
EV (Expected Value)
A quick way to measure whether a bet looks worth taking. Positive EV means the model thinks the price may be better than it should be.
Model Probability
Model Probability
The model’s estimate of how often a bet should win.
Spread
Spread
The margin a team must cover. Favorites usually have a negative number and underdogs usually have a positive number.
Moneyline
Moneyline
A bet on which team will win the game outright.
Total
Total
A bet on whether the combined score will go over or under the sportsbook’s number.
Implied Probability The chance of an outcome based on the sportsbook’s listed odds before removing sportsbook margin. Vig-Free Probability A cleaner estimate of each team’s true market probability after removing the sportsbook’s built-in margin.
